Who We Are

The Crafted Cravings company was established in 2025 by founder Jean Redding, who was committed to bringing personalization to each item served to a customer. After all, an order built for a customer should have their full input shine through! In line with that philosophy, Crafted Cravings gives the customer the full freedom to customize their sandwich order to their desire. The model was inspired by buffet-style eating, but without the labor of needing to assemble everything yourself. The ingredients used by Crafted Cravings are sourced from local suppliers that focus on providing the most organic product possible. Each sandwich is handmade by Crafted Craving's sandwich artisans that channel the input of the customer into their work. We hope you enjoy eating with us!
